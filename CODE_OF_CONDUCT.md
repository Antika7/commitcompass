# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in CommitSense a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Show empathy toward other contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Reporting

Report issues by opening a GitHub issue or contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be handled promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove contributions or ban participants who violate these standards, at their discretion.
